Our Story

The Roots of Gardenia Dreams

Where Blooms Blossom and Dreams Flourish Gardenia Dreams was born from the love of two nature Kodegoers, Ann Marie and Precious, during the Kodego MP2 project. Our mission? To bring the beauty of nature into your life through exquisite flowers, thoughtful gifts, and unforgettable moments. Every bouquet, plant, and keepsake at Gardenia Dreams tells a story and creates lasting memories. Join us on this floral adventure and let Gardenia Dreams be a part of your story.
