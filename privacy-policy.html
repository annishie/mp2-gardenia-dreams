Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us at Gardenia Dreams. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. By using our website, you consent to the practices described in this policy.


1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information when you interact with our website:

Contact Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
Payment Information: Your credit card or other payment details when making a purchase.
Account Information: Usernames, passwords, and account preferences.
Usage Information: Details about how you use our website, including pages viewed and products searched for.
Communication Information: Correspondence, such as emails or chat logs, when you contact our customer support.


2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for various purposes, including but not limited to:

Processing and fulfilling orders
Providing customer support
Improving our website and services
Communicating with you about promotions, products, and updates


3. Security

We take data security seriously and have implemented measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data, and you transmit your information at your own risk.


4. Cookies

We use cookies to collect information about your browsing preferences. You can choose to accept or decline cookies, although this may affect your experience on our website.


5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. Please review the privacy policies of those websites.


6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please don't hesitate to contact us. Our customer support team is here to assist you.
